üöÄ Phase 1 Capstone Project

Student E-commerce Behavior Analysis

The goal is to apply all the skills learned in Phase 1 to a real-world scenario. You will act as a data analyst for a student-focused e-commerce platform.

  • Data Handling: Import and thoroughly clean raw student shopping data from a CSV file.
  • Analysis: Use Pandas to analyze top product categories, peak purchase times, and spending patterns.
  • Visualization: Use Matplotlib and Seaborn to create compelling charts visualizing top-selling products and key trends.
  • Reporting: Generate a final report or a simple interactive dashboard that summarizes key insights for business stakeholders.
